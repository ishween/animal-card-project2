Starfish

Starfish have no brain and no blood, they can regenerate.

  • Scientific Name: Asteroidea
  • Average Length: five to ten inches
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Habitat: coral

Starfish belongs to a large group of marine animals called echinoderms. Largest populations of starfish live in the Indian and Pacific oceans. Starfish are usually located in the shallow water.
